Frequently Asked Questions

What is Enmojo?

Enmojo is your home's new best friend. We are a team of energy experts that are here to help guide you through the often difficult energy efficiency related projects you may encounter. From free energy audits to solar installations, we can help educate and empower you to make the best decision for your family and get connected to trusted professionals who can help.

Why should I use Enmojo?

Making your home more energy efficient is not always easy. There are lots of great things you can do, but choosing which ones to do and with which companies to work with can often times be daunting. Our experts will prioritize your projects, help you navigate the complex landscape and connect you with only the most highly trusted professionals. You don't need to be an expert, we've got that covered!

Is this service free?

Yes, 100%.

If I give you my information, will you annoy me with lots of phone calls?

No. We want to be helpful not bothersome. If at any time that is not the case, just let us know and we will never bother you again….promise.

What can Enmojo do for my business?

Enmojo is a tool you can use to help your customers become more energy efficient. We have spoken with thousands of homeowners and it is pretty clear to us that customers want to know more about things such as free energy audits and solar. They just don't know where to go. Now, you can build off of the trust you've established with your customer base and help them get on the path to efficiency. You will look good and you will get paid. Sounds good, right?

Why should my business use Enmojo?

It's easy, quick and free. Download our free mobile app Hooper and start referring customers today! We only work with highly vetted professionals so you can feel good about helping your customers not bothering them. Oh, and did we mention you will get paid?

Why should I feel comfortable recommending Enmojo to my customers?

We're here to help, not annoy. Our customers like our free service and they like that we know our stuff. We don't pester or annoy, again we are here to help. Enmojo will help you build even more trust with your customers. We are here for you and for them.

Do you work with everyone?

No. We only work with the best. That applies to both the companies we refer to and the companies we get referrals from. Are you Enmojo quality? Find out now.
