About Us

Why we do what we do.

At Enmojo, we believe that becoming energy efficient should not be difficult task. In fact, with current technologies, favorable policy and generous tax incentives, making homes more efficient has truly become a "no brainer." Why then, is it that most homeowners are confused and frustrated when it comes time to make these critical choices? Lack of product knowledge and industry fragmentation have led to a landscape that is overwhelming for most. Enmojo, changes this paradigm.

Enmojo is an energy efficiency customer acquisition company. Using our proprietary technology and techniques we empower homeowners with the knowledge they need to make informed decisions and connect them with highly trusted efficiency partners. It's simple. We believe that by making it easier to become efficient we can drive consumer behavior while delivering highly engaged customers to companies in need.

Our team has spent thousands of hours in homes selling energy efficiency. Whether it be insulation, heating and cooling or even solar, we've identified certain tactics that have proven extremely effective. We've seen first-hand what drives consumer adoption and are taking the techniques we've perfected to produce highly engaging and scalable technologies with broad applications.

Enmojo is an energy efficiency customer acquisition company that uses proprietary customer engagement techniques to deliver highly qualified and actionable customers to energy efficiency service providers.
